The purpose of this design system is to document patterns and components specific to the e-Referral service.

Typography

Frutiger is the NHS brand font. Email the standards team at service-manual@nhs.net for the desktop font.

Body paragraph size is 19px as the baseline. For information dense components and patterns, such as tables, use 16px. Line height should always be 1.5 times the size of the font.

Fallback font

Fallback to Arial when Frutiger isn't available.

font-family: “Frutiger W01”, Arial, sans-serif;

Colour

We use NHS brand colours that allow consistency of NHS services, this helps people trust the service they are using.

We use colour to prioritise and differentiate between different information. For example; red for clinical safety and grey for inactivity

Our text and background colours are designed to meet accessiblity needs. We meet at least AA for contrast and aim for AAA as far as possible.
